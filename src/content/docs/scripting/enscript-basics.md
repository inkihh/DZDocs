- The type system: primitives, `string`, `vector`, arrays, maps.
- Classes, inheritance, and `modded class` — the heart of how DayZ mods extend the
  game without touching its source.
- References (`ref`), ownership, and when things get deleted.
- Modules, scopes, and how files are compiled together.
- Syntax surprises for people coming from C#, C++, or JavaScript.
